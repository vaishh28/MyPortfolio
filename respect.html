Time and Respect

One’s upon a time. I was sitting our grocery shop. That day my father test me how I behave with the customer and unfortunately I not passed it with 100% because I behave with them good but I stop them for a while as I was busy in my work. That time my father told me always attended the customer first then do our work.

    That inspire me a lot and got that message from my father. My father not shout on me they just explain me how to behave the importance of behaviour. In their shouting also love present. My parents always inspire me sometimes explaining and telling things in lovely way and sometimes by shouting on us..

    Because of this I feel like that my home is heaven.

"Customer is like God"
